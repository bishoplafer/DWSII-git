DWSII Assignment B1 Selectors

Anatomical features of a Dragon

  • The head of a camel
  • Ears like a cow
  • Fiery eyes of a demon
  • Horns of a deer
  • Long neck like a serpent
  • Scales of a carp
  • Belly of a frog
  • Paws of a tiger (3-5 toes)
  • Claws of an eagle (3-5 claws)
  • A long beard
  • Short and long spines on the back
  • Foot rule (wierd object on the head)
  • Folded wings
  • Long tendril like whiskers or antannae
  • Color from greenish to golden
  • A bright pearl
